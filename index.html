The problem

Sending money abroad
is still broken.

Traditional systems were built for a static world. Today, global movement of money is demanded every second — and the infrastructure hasn't caught up.

Slow Transfers
Money gets stuck in legacy correspondent banking loops for days, not minutes. Your family waits. Your business stalls.
Hidden Markups
Vague exchange rates and mystery fees drain value silently. By the time money arrives, 5–8% has vanished into someone else's pocket.
Zero Transparency
Unknown outcomes and no real-time tracking. A "send and pray" approach to something that deeply matters to people.
Recipient Friction
Recipients are forced to download new apps or open new accounts just to receive money. That should never happen.

The solution

Intelligent routing.
Zero friction.

Our payment orchestration layer dynamically selects the best route for every transaction based on cost, speed, and real-time reliability — in milliseconds.

Auto-Route Switching

If a provider stalls or latency spikes, Rheo instantly re-routes through an alternative path. Your transfer keeps moving, always.

Liquidity Optimisation

A central USDC hub and pre-funded local currency pools ensure money is always available to settle instantly in the destination country.

Native Bank Delivery

Recipients never need the Rheo app. Funds arrive directly in their existing local bank account — just like a domestic transfer.

The core stack

Infrastructure first.

Transaction Engine
A high-throughput immutable ledger handling cross-border settlements with atomic finality. Every cent tracked. Nothing lost.
Routing Mesh
A decision engine that scores every provider on cost, speed, reliability, and liquidity — then picks the winner in milliseconds.
Liquidity Layer
USDC-backed liquidity pools in each corridor, pre-funded and continuously rebalanced so money is always available to settle.
Compliance Engine
Real-time KYC, AML, sanctions screening, and Travel Rule compliance baked into every single transaction automatically.
